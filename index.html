La Nostra Storia

A Family Table in the Heart of Brașov

Nestled inside the historic Hotel Casa Wagner on Piața George Enescu Nr16, 500031 Brașov, Romania, Dei Frati has welcomed guests since 2015 with elevated Italian cuisine, homemade pasta, fine wine and the warmth of a true family kitchen.

  • Atmosphere: Casual, Historic, Romantic, Upmarket, Cosy, Quiet & Trendy — perfect for groups and tourists alike.
  • Popular for: Lunch, Solo dining & Dinner — with brunch, dessert and full table service.
  • Price range: lei 60–140 ($) · Opens at 12 PM daily.

Offerings

Alcohol, cocktails, late-night food, small plates, vegan & vegetarian options, wine, beer, coffee, quick bite, spirits.

Dining Options

Brunch, lunch, dinner & dessert with comfortable seating and attentive full table service.

Family & Pets

Good for kids with high chairs available. Dogs are welcome both inside and outside.

Accessibility

Wheelchair-accessible seating and entrance. Please note: no wheelchair-accessible car park.

Amenities

Bar on site & toilet. A snug spot for an aperitivo before dinner or a nightcap after.

Planning & Payments

Reservations recommended for brunch, lunch & dinner — usually a wait. Accepts credit cards, debit cards & NFC mobile payments.

Parking & Crowd

Paid street parking nearby. A favourite of groups and tourists exploring old-town Brașov.
